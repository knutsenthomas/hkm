Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to universal design

His Kingdom Ministry wants our websites and content to be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability.

Our Goal

We work continuously to meet the requirements for universal design of ICT (WCAG 2.1). We believe that God's word and our community should be open to everyone, and digital barriers should not stand in the way of this.

Conformance Status

Partial conformance: We are aware that some parts of our website may not fully satisfy all requirements in WCAG 2.1 level AA yet, but we are actively working on improvements.

What we do for accessibility

We have focused on the following measures to ensure good accessibility:

  • Contrast and readability: We use clear colors with good contrast between text and background to ensure that the text is easy to read.
  • Keyboard navigation: The website is built so that it is possible to navigate in menus and content using the keyboard (Tab key).
  • Images and media: We are working on including text alternatives (alt text) for images that carry information.
  • Video and teaching: We aim to subtitle our teaching videos so that they are accessible to the hearing impaired and those who watch video without sound.

Known limitations

We are aware of the following areas that we are working to improve:

  • Older video content may lack subtitles.
  • Some documents (PDF) may not be optimized for screen readers.

Give us feedback

Do you experience problems using our website, or do you have suggestions for improvements? We greatly appreciate your feedback.

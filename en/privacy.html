Privacy Policy

Home Privacy

At His Kingdom Ministry ("we", "us"), we are committed to ensuring that you feel safe when visiting our websites and using our services. This privacy policy explains in a simple and clear way what information we collect about you, why we do it, and what rights you have.

As a Christian organization based on trust and fellowship, we treat your data with the utmost respect and confidentiality.

1. What information do we collect?

We only collect information that is necessary to give you access to our courses, teaching, and community.

  • When registering on "My Account": Name, email address, and password (encrypted).
  • When using the course portal: Information about which courses you attend, and your progression (e.g., which videos you have finished watching). We do this so that you can easily continue where you left off.
  • When registering for events: Contact information and any dietary needs (if you provide this voluntarily).

2. How do we use the information?

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To give you access to your personal profile and course material.
  • To send you relevant information about courses you participate in.
  • To improve our services and websites based on general usage statistics (anonymized).
  • To maintain the security of your user account.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the website functions optimally.

Necessary cookies: These are strictly necessary for you to be able to log in and navigate safely on "My Account". These cannot be opted out of.

Statistics and analysis: We may use tools to understand how the website is used, so that we can make it better. You can choose to opt out of these.

You can change your cookie settings at any time via the link at the bottom of the website.

4. Storage and deletion

Your personal data is stored securely in our database (provided by Google Firebase). We retain your information for as long as you have an active user account with us.

You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us, or by using the "Delete account" function inside your profile.

5. Sharing of information

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

If you make a donation or purchase a course, your payment information will be processed directly by our payment provider (e.g., Vipps or Stripe). We do not store card numbers or bank information ourselves.

6. Your rights

According to GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), you have the right to:

  • Access the information we have stored about you.
  • Correction of incorrect information.
  • Deletion of your information ("the right to be forgotten").
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

7. Contact us

If you have questions about privacy or wish to exercise your rights, you are welcome to contact us:

Email: post@hiskingdomministry.no
Address: Norway

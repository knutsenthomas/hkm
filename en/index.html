Welcome to the community

We are a non-profit organization

His Kingdom Ministry conducts spiritual gatherings such as prayer meetings, teaching seminars, and missionary travel. We want to be a community where people can grow in their faith and relationship with Jesus.

Our Mission

To equip and inspire people to a deeper life with God through teaching, community, and prayer. We want to be a place where people can grow in their faith.

Our History

Started with a vision to bring people together in spiritual growth, we have grown into a vibrant community that conducts prayer meetings, teaching, and travel.

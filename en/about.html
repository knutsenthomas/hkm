Who We Are

His Kingdom Ministry

His Kingdom Ministry is a Christian organization based in Lyngdal. We conduct teaching, prayer, and missions, and want to help people grow in their faith. Among other things, we have a podcast with weekly devotionals, and we arrange various gatherings and seminars.

Contact Us
Our History

Our Foundation & Story

Our Foundation

His Kingdom Ministry focuses on missions, equipping the saints, Bible teaching, prayer, intercession, healing and deliverance, and growing in the prophetic gifts of the Spirit. Everything we do will be done by Biblical standards and God’s guidance.

The Story Behind

Hilde Karin started going on mission and Bible smuggling trips when she was 14 and has since lived as a missionary in the Middle East, Africa, and Spain. In May 2022, God called her back home to Norway, where she met Thomas. They were married on December 2, 2023. Thomas has worked for 16 years in Church Administration/Accounting. Together, they have visited 9 countries, driven by a heart for the lost.

From Foundation to 2.0

Before Hilde Karin met Thomas, she had an organization called His Kingdom Foundation (received in prayer in 2008). His Kingdom Ministry was registered while on their honeymoon as a "2.0 upgrade," holding the same high value: that everything we do will be done by Biblical standards and God’s guidance.

His Kingdom Designs

The organization runs His Kingdom Designs, offering products in Norwegian, English, and Spanish for gifts/evangelism while funding our ministry. Hilde Karin designs the products and Thomas is the webdesigner. Our vision is to help and bless other designers to sell their creations!

Our Team

Meet the hearts behind His Kingdom Ministry

Hilde Karin Knutsen

Prophetic Intercessor and Teacher

Welcome to my page. My name is Hilde Karin. I am married to Thomas. We live in Norway. I have traveled as a missionary and prophetic intercessor for more than half my life. By God's grace, through Biblical principles and by the Holy Spirit's leading, I am a prophetic voice ready to help you grow in your personal relationship with Jesus Christ and equip you to become everything He has made you to be. I love to see God's work manifesting in your life, whether it's miracles, inner and physical healing, salvation etc. I love to see you become free from all bondage, and be able to walk with God and manifest His life in and through you. I love to see you grow in your relationship with and faith in Jesus Christ, and I would love to teach you more about living a life of prayer, how you can hear God more clearly and see your prayers answered.

Thomas Knutsen

CEO and Worship

Hello and welcome to our page. We are so glad you came to our ministry site. I have not traveled around the world. I have worked and lived mostly in the same city my whole life. I worked in the same church for 15 years before I met Hilde. We connected right away and are so blessed that God united our lives together. I have worked in many different departments in the church, such as children, youth, worship, and administration. I have been a worship leader since I was 15 years old and love to worship God. I am responsible for everything technical in His Kingdom Ministry from the website to video and podcast editing. I believe that God will change many lives through this ministry.
